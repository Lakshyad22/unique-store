Returns and Exchange Policy

Returns Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your order details.

Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for items of the same value within 30 days of purchase. To be eligible for an exchange, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer support team with your order details.

Shipping Policy

We offer free shipping for all orders over Rs.999 For orders under Rs.999, a flat shipping fee of Rs.95 will be applied.
