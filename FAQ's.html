Unique Clothing Store - FAQs

What makes Unique Clothing Store different from other clothing stores?

Unique Clothing Store specializes in offering exclusive Indian streetwear that you won't find anywhere else. Our designs are inspired by the rich cultural heritage of India, blended with contemporary styles to create truly unique fashion statements.

Do you offer international shipping?

No, we don't offer international shipping but we do offer shipping all over India.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order directly through our website by adding items to your cart and proceeding to checkout. Alternatively, you can also place orders through our mobile app.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through various secure methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and cash on delivery (COD) for select locations within India.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping on orders above a certain value within India. Please refer to our shipping policy for more details.
