Shipping Information

Shipping Policy

At Unique, we strive to provide efficient and reliable shipping services to our customers. Our shipping policy is designed to ensure timely delivery of your orders while maintaining the quality of our products.

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery within 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: Estimated delivery within 2-3 business days
    • A

Shipping Rates

Shipping rates are calculated based on the weight of your order and the shipping method selected. Rates will be displayed at checkout before you confirm your order.

Track Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your order on our website or through our shipping carrier's website.
